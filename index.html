Grilled Cheese Recipe!

Learn how to make a simple Grilled Cheese Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Cheese
  • Margarine

Instructions

  1. Turn stove to medium heat.
  2. Lay out two slices of bread.
  3. Spread margarine on one side of each bread.
  4. Place one slice of bread margarine side down on the stove.
  5. Put the slice of cheese on top.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top of the cheese, margarine side up.
  7. Let bread rest on stove until golden brown.
  8. Flip the sandwich and do the same for the second side.
  9. Then flip and compress bread for maximum cheesiness on both sides until your liking.
